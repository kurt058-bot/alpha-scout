🧪 Risk Profile Backtest v.1.0
How this works: your current portfolio allocation is held static and projected backward, compared against the benchmarks you select. For any holding younger than the backtest start date, its pre-inception history is approximated using a long-history proxy ETF matched to that fund's category (e.g. a small-value fund launched in 2023 borrows Russell 2000 Value's actual 2000–2023 returns to fill the gap), rebased to connect seamlessly where the fund's real data begins. This approximates the fund's asset-class risk profile, not its exact factor tilts — it's the same technique fund providers use for "since inception" comparisons on young funds. Every substitution is disclosed below the results.
